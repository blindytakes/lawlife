The Attorney

Meet Chase
Keibler

Chase Keibler grew up in a blue-collar town outside Pittsburgh before relocating to South Carolina. He's a proven trial attorney with experience on both sides of the courtroom — representing injured individuals, corporate plaintiffs, and insurance companies.

That insider knowledge of how insurance companies value cases and treat injured people is exactly why he founded Keibler Law Group: to build the kind of firm he'd want representing his own family.

Chase is licensed in both North Carolina and South Carolina and has successfully tried numerous jury and bench trials to verdict — from multi-million dollar antitrust cartels to personal injury cases.
